The making

Fire first,
then patience.

  1. Carve

    Each design begins as a hand-carved wax — the only version of the piece that will ever be discarded.

  2. Cast

    Lost-wax casting in small crucibles. Gold melts at 1,064 °C; patience does not.

  3. Set

    Stones are seated one by one under the loupe, checked against the light, reseated until right.

  4. Polish

    Mirror finish by hand and wheel. If it doesn't reflect your face, it isn't done.
