Chapter 03 — the craft

The details carry the load.

What we rebuild, not replace

F·04

Pulled eyelets

Brass rings tear out of tired leather. We re-bush each one and reinforce the facing behind it.

F·05

Dried, cracking leather

Cracks start where you crease. Cleaned, conditioned, re-dyed and sealed back to depth.

F·06

Frayed laces & tongues

Waxed replacements cut to length; padded tongues re-stitched, not patched.
